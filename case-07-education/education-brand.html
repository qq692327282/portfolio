05 — Brand Elements

Design Principles

The building blocks that make Little Sparks feel warm, trustworthy, and playful.

🌟

Spark Motif

Our signature spark/flame shape appears throughout the brand — in the logo, illustrations, and decorative elements — symbolizing the light of learning.

⭕

Rounded Shapes

All corners are rounded — from buttons (50px radius) to cards (28px) to avatars (50%). No sharp edges means a safe, friendly feel.

📸

Warm Photography

Photos use warm tones, natural lighting, and always show children engaged and smiling. Soft vignettes and warm filters maintain brand warmth.

🎭

Playful Illustrations

CSS-crafted shapes and friendly characters. Our mascot "Sparky" is a warm yellow blob with a flame top — curious, happy, and always learning.

😊 🎨 📚 🔬
💬

Friendly Tone

Copy is conversational, encouraging, and inclusive. We speak to parents as partners and to children as capable, creative individuals.

"You can do it!"
📐

Generous Spacing

Plenty of white space keeps designs feeling calm and organized. Children's content doesn't need to be cluttered — clarity builds trust.
